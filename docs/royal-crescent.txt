The Royal Crescent was designed in 1839 by the architect Robert Cantwell, inspired by its famous namesake in Bath. Its Grade II* listed houses have white stucco facades and pillared porticos. From here, Shepherd’s Bush station is a short walk away, while Holland Park station is also close. In addition, Westfield shopping centre is nearby, and the open spaces of Holland Park are within easy reach.
